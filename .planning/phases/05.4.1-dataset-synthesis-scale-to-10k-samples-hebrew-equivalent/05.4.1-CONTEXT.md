## What We Need to Build

### 1. English Dataset Expansion (1K → 10K)

**Goal:** Generate 9,000 additional high-quality English samples

**Approach:**
- Use GPT-4 or Claude to generate variations of existing samples
- Maintain semantic diversity (paraphrase, context changes, term substitutions)
- Preserve label distribution (~20% appropriate, ~80% problematic)
- Ensure no duplicates or near-duplicates

**Quality Requirements:**
- Each sample must be unique (cosine similarity < 0.85)
- Maintain academic tone and context
- Cover diverse domains (psychology, sociology, medicine, education, law)
- Include edge cases and subtle problematic language

### 2. Hebrew Dataset Creation (10K samples)

**Goal:** Create culturally-appropriate Hebrew equivalent dataset

**Approach:**
- Translate English dataset (machine + human review)
- Adapt culturally (Hebrew-specific LGBTQ+ terminology)
- Validate with native Hebrew speakers
- Ensure proper encoding (UTF-8) and RTL compatibility

**Quality Requirements:**
- Natural Hebrew phrasing (not literal translation)
- Hebrew LGBTQ+ community terminology
- Academic register appropriate for Israeli academia
- Proper gender agreement and verb conjugation

### 3. Quality Validation

**Quality Metrics:**
- Semantic diversity score > 0.7 (average pairwise cosine distance)
- Zero exact duplicates
- Class balance within ±2% of target
- Manual review of 100 random samples shows >95% quality

   - Mitigation: Batch processing, use efficient models, cache results
